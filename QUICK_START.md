# 🏀 Quick Start - Basketball Stats Tracker

## Get Started in 3 Steps

### 1️⃣ Login or Register
- **New User?** Click the "Register" tab and create an account
- **Just Testing?** Click "Continue as Guest"
- **Returning?** Login with your email and password

### 2️⃣ Create Your First Game
1. Click **"Start New Game"** on the dashboard
2. Enter team names (e.g., "Lakers" vs "Bulls")
3. Add players to each team
4. Click **"Start Game"**

### 3️⃣ Track Stats Live
- Use **+** and **-** buttons to track:
  - 🏀 Points (auto-updates score)
  - 🔄 Rebounds
  - 🤝 Assists
  - ⚠️ Fouls
- Top performer gets a 🏆 trophy icon
- Click **"Finish"** when game ends

## Tips & Tricks

💡 **Top Players**: Look for the 🏆 icon next to your best performers
